About Honkai: Star Rail


Honkai: Star Rail, a role-playing gacha video game developed by miHoYo,
published by miHoYo in China and by its subsidiary HoYoverse worldwide.
It follows the gameplay style of classic Japanese role-playing games:
build up a lineup of characters, and control a team of four in turn-based combat.
Elements of open-world and dungeon exploration are present,
with multiple mechanics, including the gacha system.


Set in a science-fantasy universe in which humanity follows the paths of beings
called Aeons. The Trailblazer, is an artificial humanoid who finds themself implanted
with a seed of ruin by the god of destruction. This extreme set of circumstances is par
for the course as far as the stories of the heroes go. So when they're rescued
by the crew of the Astral Express, they choose to join them on their journey
into the unknown in search for answers about their predicament.


Other major factions include the Stellaron hunters; and the crew of the
Herta Space Station, where the Astral Express trade artifacts from their
voyages, and which is also where the Trailblazer's journey begins.
